Planet info

  • Size: 50,724 kilometers

  • Distance from the sun: 2.9 billion kilometer

  • Composition:Uranus is an ice giant, with an atmosphere rich in hydrogen, helium, and methane, and a mantle of water, ammonia, and methane ices.

  • Interesting facts:

    • Uranus rotates on its side with an axial tilt of 98 degrees.
    • It has faint rings and 27 known moons.
    • Its methane content gives it a blue-green color.
